Top Travel Destinations in Maharastra

The prehistoric temples, romantic hill stations, sandy beaches and the rolling green hills will leave you amazed in Maharashtra. On one hand you get to see the bustling metropolis and on the other an untouched paradise yearning to be explored. One among the top tourist places in India, Maharashtra promises avid travelers with unforgettable holidays. Travelers wishing to soak in the natural beauty of the mesmeric mountains can take the roads to the charming hill stations in Maharashtra. Panchgani, an extremely picturesque location will make you skip a beat. Signifying five hills, this spot is unthinkably scenic, showcasing mountains in the backdrop and coastal plains at one side. Soothing and serene, Panchgani is apt for travelers seeking solace. Akola, the heritage city is a historic and ancient spot, which is a must visit. A melting pot of Muslim, Hindu and British influence, this city is worth exploring. Wander past its splendid forts and archaic temples, a visit to this city will get etched in your time. Mumbai, the city of dreams and the capital city of Maharashtra is the most sought after tourism places of India. Embracing people belonging to any religion and culture, it is a hub of dynamism. Being world famous for the Hindi Film Industry i.e. “Bollywood”, theatre, literary and artistic activities, it is an ultimate city to discover. While taking a tour of this place, don’t miss to visit few of the prominent sites like Elephanta Caves, Gateway of India and Taj Hotel. Other places worth witnessing are Worli Fort, Juhu Beach, Chor Bazar and Haji Ali. The list is unending as there are numerous tourist places in Maharashtra, which you just can’t miss out Amravati City, Diveagar Coast, Tuljapur, Kudal town, Ratnagiri, Chikhaldara hill station and City of Kohlapur are few places that will leave you bewitched. Your Maharashtra tour will be incomplete if you miss to halt at Kolad. Its scenic marshlands, gurgling waterfalls and captivating valleys are wonders worth witnessing. Apart from sightseeing, here adventure freaks can also indulge in wild and whacky adventure sports like river crossing, kayaking and paragliding. The one who wishes to take a plunge in the beauty of nature, Kolad is ‘’the’’ place to be in. Kamshet is another most renowned spot to indulge in hardcore adventure sports. If you are one among the daunting adventure enthusiast, enjoy world class paragliding here. Kamshet, being a quaint location, bounded by shimmering lakes and lush green hills will serve no less than a dreamy getaway. If you are looking for leisure and utmost merriment, Alibaug also referred to as the Goa of Maharashtra is an enchanting town. Adorned with a charming coastline, sparkling sea and golden beaches, this is one of the most striking tourist’s hotspot that a traveler must wander past. Not forgetting the wildlife lovers, they can quench their thirst for travel by halting at enticing wildlife spots like Tadoba National Park, Bhimashankar and Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary. There is lot more that Maharashtra holds for all classes of travelers. All one need to do is to journey to this place packed with bounty of nature, adventure, fun and merriment.
